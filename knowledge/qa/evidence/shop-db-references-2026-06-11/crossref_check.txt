Legacy `<type>-<slug>-YYYY-MM-DD.md` names remain accepted everywhere (dual-format tolerance) for historical artifacts — never use them for new deposits to watched directories. Non-watched locations (roadmaps, research, architecture docs) keep descriptive+date naming.
