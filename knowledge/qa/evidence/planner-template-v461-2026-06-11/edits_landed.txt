── E7 — Commit lookup by id tag ──
  NEW 'id-based lookup via' present in PT: True
    660:**Dev-log commit SHA self-reference is structurally impossible.** When authoring a DEV step that commits a dev log, do NOT require the dev log to embed its own commit SHA. A commit's SHA is computed over its full tree contents including the dev-log text; editing the dev log to insert the SHA produces a new tree, which produces a new SHA, which does not match the embedded value. The amend loop cannot converge. Two acceptable patterns: (a) defer SHA capture to QA — the DEV step commits the dev log with a placeholder or omits the SHA, and the QA step records the stabilized DEV commit SHA in the QA report (which is a separate commit and does not face the self-reference problem); (b) omit the embedded SHA entirely and rely on id-based lookup via `git log --grep='\[<id>\]'` (the `[<id>]` commit tag Bellows injects into every agent prompt; slug-grep remains for legacy plans) or `git log --oneline -- <changed-file>`. Plans that instruct the DEV agent to "amend the dev log to include the commit SHA" are authoring errors and produce stranded amend loops.
