## Background

The 2026-05-06 BACKLOG entry on `PlanHandler._seen` documents that the path-keyed cache prevents re-dispatch of plans returning to a previously-seen path string, forcing a full daemon restart on retry workflows. The diagnostic at `bellows/knowledge/research/plan-handler-seen-retry-cache-2026-05-11.md` characterized the lifecycle (3 terminal-event call sites: continue-to-done at line 1027, halt at line 1047, auto-close at line 527), confirmed the dispatch-window race is real and load-bearing, and recommended Option (B): move `_seen` ownership onto `Bellows`. This plan ships Option (B) with slug-only keying (no dispatch-attempt-id) and adds lifecycle-event discards.
